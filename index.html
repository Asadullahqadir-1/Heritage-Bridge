Our Team

The people behind your investment

Dr. Sarah Kamau

CEO & Co-Founder

With over 15 years in East African investment banking and advisory, Sarah has facilitated more than $50M in cross-border transactions. A former Senior Advisor at the African Development Bank, she holds a PhD in Development Economics from Oxford University and is passionate about empowering diaspora communities to invest with confidence.

James Omondi

Director of Real Estate

A former official at the Kenya Land Registry with 12 years of experience, James specialises in property acquisition, title verification, and land due diligence across Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda. He has overseen over 300 successful transactions for diaspora investors navigating local property markets from abroad.

Grace Uwimana

Rwanda Country Director

Grace brings a decade of experience from the Rwanda Development Board, where she supported foreign and diaspora investment initiatives. A trusted bridge between international investors and Rwanda's rapidly growing economy, she provides on-the-ground insight, regulatory guidance, and partner network access for our Rwanda-based clients.

Michael Adeyemi

US Diaspora Relations Lead

Based in Washington D.C., Michael has spent a decade helping African diaspora communities across North America structure and execute investments back on the continent. His expertise spans cross-border legal compliance, financial structuring, and building lasting relationships between US-based investors and East African partners.
